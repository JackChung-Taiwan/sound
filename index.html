How to Play

1. Select your Pokémon partner.

Movement: "Up", "Down", "Left", "Right"

🔥 Combo Mode: Say multiple commands at once! (e.g., "Up up left")

Visuals: "Red", "Green", "Blue", or "Reset"

Size: "Big", "Small", or "Normal"

Action: "Jump" to leap, or "Don't Jump" to cancel.

遊戲說明

1. 從左上角召喚你的寶可夢夥伴。

移動指令： 說 "Up", "Down", "Left", "Right" 走迷宮。

🔥 連續技模式：你可以一口氣連走好幾步！(例如說："Up up left")

視覺指令： 說 "Red", "Green", "Blue", 或 "Reset" 變色。

尺寸指令： 說 "Big", "Small", 或 "Normal" 改變大小。

特殊動作： 說 "Jump" 原地跳躍，或說 "Don't Jump" 取消。
