How to Play

1. Select your Pokémon partner.

Movement: "Go Up", "Go Down", "Go Left", "Go Right" to navigate the maze.

Visuals: "Red", "Green", "Blue", or "Reset" to change colors.

Size: "Big", "Small", or "Normal".

Action: "Jump" to leap in place, or "Don't Jump" to cancel.

遊戲說明

1. 召喚你的寶可夢夥伴。

移動指令： 說 "Go Up", "Go Down", "Go Left", "Go Right" 在迷宮中移動。

視覺指令： 說 "Red", "Green", "Blue", 或 "Reset" 改變顏色。

尺寸指令： 說 "Big", "Small", 或 "Normal" 改變大小。

特殊動作： 說 "Jump" 原地跳躍，或說 "Don't Jump" 取消。
