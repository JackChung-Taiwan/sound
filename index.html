How to Play

1. Select a Pokémon from the top menu.

Movement: Say "Go Up", "Go Down", "Go Left", "Go Right" (or just the direction).

Visuals: Say "Red", "Green", "Blue", or "Reset" to change colors.

Size: Say "Big", "Small", or "Normal".

Action: Say "Jump" to leap, or "Don't Jump" to cancel.

* Speak clearly and loud!

遊戲說明

1. 從頂部選單召喚你的寶可夢夥伴。

移動指令： 說 "Go Up", "Go Down", "Go Left", "Go Right" (只說單字也可以喔！)

視覺指令： 說 "Red", "Green", "Blue", 或 "Reset" 改變顏色。

尺寸指令： 說 "Big", "Small", 或 "Normal" 改變大小。

特殊動作： 說 "Jump" 讓角色跳躍，或說 "Don't Jump" 取消跳躍。

* 大膽開口，唸錯了電腦也會包容你的！
